Multiple OS

The inField™ is capable of running on leading mobile OSes, namely Android, iOS, Windows®, and even on Symbian. This multi OS support provides freedom to the feet-on-street to have a phone of their choice and still can report to their managers without hassle.

Analytics you need

Managers receive real-time and relevant reports of the activities of their FOS(Feet-on-street) so that they can make more effective plans and targets.

Capture proofs

The inField™ enables the FOS to collect proofs of their reported activities so that you can rest assured that the tasks on street level are being done. And all this takes place in real-time without any hassle.

Location based services

inField™ keeps track of the locations visited by FOS hence making you assured that your guys are doing their job. It uses GPS as well as network based location to provide the manager the maximum information and report of the FOS' whereabouts.

Works offline

Is your guy stuck in a place where connectivity is questionable?
Have few of your people got slower GPRS connections?
Our mobile apps work where there is ABSOLUTE ZERO INTERNET connectivity because people out on the streets gotta stay mobile no matter what the conditions are. inField™ can even sync data without internet connectivity hence you get real-time reports no matter how much away your guys are from the internet.
